The JSON is used in the About Us page and Contact Us page. Javascript is heavely used in the Loan Request page and Calculate Loan page. The Video is in the loan Info page.

Site Purpose

The project will be a website for a lending company. This company assists people receive loans for debt consolidation and other smaller personal loans. The website will explain the loan process and the benefits of applying. The website will have a loan calculator and you will be able to apply for a loan.

Target Audiance

he audience for this website are people seeking a loan to either pay off debt, or for a major purchase. These people want to either get in a better situation financially or pay for a purchase at a reasonable interest rate. The age range are middle age men and women from 30-50 years old. These individuals are middle class or lower. They probably have a wide variety of education levels. These individuals could visit this site from any device in many locations across the united states. They may work 0,1 or more jobs, so they may be busy and want the process to be quick and easy. They will want to understand the loan process which may be complicated in a simple way.

Persona

  • Name: Bob Smith
  • Age: 35
  • Income: Middle Class
  • Bob Smith is fairly young, but a few months ago, due to a few major purchases is now swimming in debt. He has credit card loans, medical bills, car payments and even student loans to finish paying off. He is not sure what do do, but knows he wants to pay it off. He wants to pay off this loan quickly but in a way that he can afford.
